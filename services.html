Career guidance

A career path is a reflection of aspirations, and it entails deciding what you really want to study in college or university. It entails selecting marketable courses, looking for work, and dealing with work-related issues. With one-on-one involvement from our team, the Student Career Guide provides students with expert guidance, tools, and assistance in developing and implementing their career plans. Our career services team offers a comprehensive skill-building program to help you improve your skills and position yourself for success in the job market.

Factors to consider when choosing career:-
  1. Employment Rate for Graduates.
  2. Your attitude.
  3. Personal interests.
  4. Time span-how long will it take you to complete.
  5. Feedback-Asking experts and professionals about course you want to takee.
  6. Your perfomance academically-courses such as Engineering and Medicine requires potential student
  7. Finances
