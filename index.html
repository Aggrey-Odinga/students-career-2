STUDENT CAREER GUIDE

The Students Career Guide provides students with expert guidance, tools, and assistance in developing and implementing their career plans. Many students lack the fundamental knowledge required to choose a career after completing their secondary education before enrolling in university or college. One major issue is that many of them are unfamiliar with the courses offered by higher education institutions. Another issue is that many students are unaware that simply meeting the cut-off points for admission to a university or college does not imply that they are qualified to pursue all of the courses offered by all universities or colleges. There is a limit based on your average score and the subjects you took for the KCSE exam. I believe that secondary students should be informed and trained on how to "go about it" when selecting a career path as early as possible.....

Whatever you're studying, our Career Services team has a comprehensive skill-building program that will help you improve your talents and position yourself for success in the job market. Expert advice and resources will be provided for any career you are interested in, as well as any degree you are pursuing.
